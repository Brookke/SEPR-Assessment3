Murder Mystery Game

You have been been invited to a lockin costume party with some of the best detectives around. There has been a murder, one of the detectives has killed another. Each of these detectives wants to prove their worth and solve the crime. They have each visited the murder scene and found at least one clue each, so that the other detectives can't solve the murder first, each one of them has hidden the clue in their rooms. You must go around each room trying to find the clues that the detectives have hidden. You must also question the other detectives to see if they know any more than they originally let on.
